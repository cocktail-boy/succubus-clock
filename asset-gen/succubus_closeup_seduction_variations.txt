SUCCUBUS CLOSE-UP SEDUCTION SHOTS - 10 VARIATIONS
================================================================

WORKFLOW NOTE
-------------
Each variation below is a close-up, alluring seduction shot for the
same anchor character. Keep the pose sensual and intimate without
explicit nudity or graphic sexual content. Use the CHARACTER SHEET
block verbatim in every prompt, and attach the approved anchor image
as a reference so likeness is preserved while expression, lighting,
and framing change.

Style note: no smoke in prompts; embers only.

================================================================
CHARACTER SHEET (paste verbatim into every prompt)
================================================================

Adult infernal queen, mid-twenties in apparent age. Heart-shaped
face, high cheekbones, sharp half-lidded almond eyes with deep
crimson irises, dark thick lashes, defined dark brows. Glossy
cherry-red lips, full and slightly parted. Honey-warm porcelain
skin with soft subsurface scattering, faint rose flush across
cheeks and nose. Long straight raven-black hair to mid-back with
a subtle inner sheen. Two elegant matte-black horns curving back
from her temples, ridged like polished obsidian, roughly eight
inches long. Slim black tail with a spaded tip. Broad bat wings
with deep crimson membranes and black bone structure. Outfit:
dark-crimson corset bralette with gold filigree across the bust
panel, matching obsidian micro skirt with gold trim, slim black
choker with a single ruby drop, gold filigree arm cuffs at the
biceps. Painterly cinematic fantasy game key art style.

--- 01. Ember-Lit Invitation ---
[CHARACTER SHEET] Extreme portrait close-up from the upper chest
to just above the horns, her face filling the frame. She leans
slightly toward the viewer with a slow confident smile, one hand
resting lightly at her collarbone near the ruby choker. Her wings
form a dark crimson backdrop behind her shoulders, softly out of
focus. Warm ember underlight catches the gloss of her lips and the
curve of her cheekbones; cool violet shadow shapes the far side of
her face. Intimate locked-camera composition, shallow depth of
field, tack-sharp eyes, painterly cinematic detail, embers only.

--- 02. Finger to Lips ---
[CHARACTER SHEET] Tight seductive close-up, shoulders and face
framed in a vertical composition. She lifts one delicate finger
near her parted lips in a quiet hush gesture, eyes half-lidded and
directly meeting the viewer. Her expression is playful, teasing,
and controlled rather than exaggerated. The ruby choker glints at
the base of her throat; black horns arc elegantly into the upper

in the dark background, painterly realism, sharp catchlights,
no smoke.

--- 03. Over-the-Shoulder Glance ---

behind her like a velvet curtain, deep crimson membrane catching a
thin rim of warm light. Dark gothic chamber, ember specks in the
distance, shallow depth of field, painterly cinematic focus on the
