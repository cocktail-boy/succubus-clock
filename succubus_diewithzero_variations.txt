SUCCUBUS x DIE WITH ZERO — 6 CONCEPT VARIATIONS
================================================================

WORKFLOW NOTE
-------------
Each variation below dramatizes one conceptual link between
succubus mythology and the Die with Zero philosophy. Same identity
anchor as the 20-variation set — paste the CHARACTER SHEET block
verbatim into every prompt and (ideally) attach an approved anchor
image as a reference so likeness is preserved while the scene and
mood change. Do not paraphrase the character sheet — identical
tokens across calls give the model the best chance at consistent
identity.

================================================================
CHARACTER SHEET (paste verbatim into every prompt)
================================================================

Adult infernal queen, mid-twenties in apparent age. Heart-shaped
face, high cheekbones, sharp half-lidded almond eyes with deep
crimson irises, dark thick lashes, defined dark brows. Glossy
cherry-red lips, full and slightly parted. Honey-warm porcelain
skin with soft subsurface scattering, faint rose flush across
cheeks and nose. Long straight raven-black hair to mid-back with
a subtle inner sheen. Two elegant matte-black horns curving back
from her temples, ridged like polished obsidian, roughly eight
inches long. Slim black tail with a spaded tip. Broad bat wings
with deep crimson membranes and black bone structure. Outfit:
dark-crimson corset bralette with gold filigree across the bust
panel, matching obsidian micro skirt with gold trim, slim black
choker with a single ruby drop, gold filigree arm cuffs at the
biceps. Painterly cinematic fantasy game key art style.

================================================================
VARIATION PROMPTS
================================================================

--- 01. The Life-Energy Moneylender ---
Concept: life force as the actual currency. Money is stored life,
and she is the one who knows the exchange rate.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She sits behind a polished obsidian counting
table inside a dim gothic counting-house, an open ledger before
her and an ornate brass balance scale at her elbow. On one pan of
the scale sits a neat stack of gold coins; on the other, a small
hourglass whose sand glows like molten gold. She holds a single
coin between thumb and forefinger, lifted near her cheek as she
examines it with one eye, head tilted, a small amused smirk at
her lips. Loose stacks of coins, rolled parchments, and a dripping
candle clutter the table. Wings folded close, tail curled around
the leg of her chair. Warm desk-candle key light, deep cathedral
shadow behind, embers drifting in the dark, shallow depth of
field, tack-sharp eyes, painterly cinematic detail.

--- 02. The Empty Hourglass ---
Concept: "die with zero" is literally what she does. Drained to
nothing, presented as accomplishment rather than horror.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She stands centered in a moonlit obsidian
chamber, holding up a tall slender hourglass at eye level with
both hands. The hourglass is completely empty — the last grain
has just fallen — and she regards it with a soft, satisfied
smile, head tilted slightly. Her expression is calm and pleased,
not triumphant — the look of a quiet job well done. Wings folded
relaxed behind her, tail at rest along the floor. A single shaft
of cool moonlight from a high window illuminates the glass; warm
rim light from a low brazier catches her cheekbone from the side.
Embers drift slowly in the still air. Shallow depth of field,
painterly realism, sharp catchlights in the eyes.

--- 03. The Auditor of Unspent Years ---
Concept: she's the responsible one — the indulgence is the
correct path, and saving was the sin she's here to flag.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She sits at a tall accountant's desk in a
vaulted gothic library, an enormous open ledger spread before her,
a quill poised in one hand. A pair of slim half-moon reading
spectacles rests low on her nose. She looks up over the rims
toward the viewer with a wry, slightly disapproving raised
eyebrow, mouth set in a small flat line — the expression of
someone reviewing a disappointing balance sheet. The ledger's
facing pages are labeled in elegant script: "EXPERIENCES" on the
left, "UNUSED YEARS" on the right, the right column heavy with
tallies. Stacks of unstamped receipts pile beside her elbow.
Wings folded primly, tail curled around the stool. Warm
desk-lamp pool from above, cool ambient library fill, painterly
detail, sharp eyes, shallow depth of field.

--- 04. Bedside Visitor ---
Concept: succubi strike during sleep — and the Die-with-Zero
warning is that sleepwalking through your prime years is the
actual loss.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She sits on the edge of a wide canopy bed in a
dim chamber where an unseen figure sleeps beneath heavy dark
sheets (only the gentle curve of a blanket suggests them — no
face shown, no body visible). She is turned three-quarters toward
the viewer rather than toward the sleeper, holding a small ornate
hourglass cupped in both palms, regarding it with a melancholy
half-smile. Predawn blue light filters through tall arched
windows behind her; a guttered candle on the nightstand has long
since burned out, only the faintest ember left. Wings folded
close against her back, tail draped quietly along the bedframe.
Cool blue rim light from the window, last warm ember-glow from
the candle stub, hushed atmosphere, painterly realism, shallow
depth of field.

--- 05. Vanitas with a Smile ---
Concept: classic memento mori, but tender. Death as the thing
that gives the present its weight, greeted as an old friend
rather than feared.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She sits at a small round table draped in deep
velvet inside a candlelit alcove, a classical vanitas arrangement
laid out before her: a polished ivory skull, an hourglass tipped
on its side, a wilting deep-crimson rose with petals fallen
across the cloth, and an extinguished black candle still warm.
She cradles the skull gently in both hands, lifted near her face,
regarding it with a soft tender smile — affectionate, almost
fond, the way one greets a long-known friend. No predatory edge
to the expression. Wings folded close, tail curled around the
chair leg. Warm low candlelight from off-frame, deep velvet
shadow behind, painterly Dutch-master chiaroscuro, shallow depth
of field, sharp catchlights in the eyes.

--- 06. The Clock-Keeper ---
Concept: the unstated countdown made explicit — the succubus
relationship always had a hidden clock; here she shows it to you.
This is the cleanest visual tie to the app itself.

[CHARACTER SHEET] She stands beside an enormous mechanical clock
face set into the ruined cathedral wall, its black iron hands
taller than she is, its numerals carved in glowing crimson
sigils. She rests one gloved hand against the long minute hand
with quiet authority, the other relaxed at her side. She turns
her head toward the viewer with a calm direct gaze and a small
knowing smile — the expression of someone showing you something
you already half-suspected. The clock's heavy brass pendulum
swings behind her, catching glints of light. Wings spread
half-open like a dark banner across the clock face, tail curled
at her heel. Warm ember underlight from below, cool moonlight
from above through the broken ceiling, dramatic painterly
composition, shallow depth of field, sharp eyes.
